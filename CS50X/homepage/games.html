A rundown through my gaming history

So, a bit of a history lesson with my life included, I choose 3 games to focus on with a bit of backstory.

Beginning of PC gaming

Even though, I was gaming since I was 5 years old, Cod Mw2 from 2009 was my first game on steam and my first game on my own personal computer. In 2010, my Dad was able to buy me a completely new computer as the old one we had was still running a pentium 4 and a geforce with 128MB VRAM. Thanks to this new one, I was able to play the latest titles, and more specially, cod mw2 online, which led into a friendship that would last some years, and I have a lot of fond memories playing with that particular friend.

The middle

This game made me think about something, I might have never connected, games are the future of art it's a combination of everything we create, music, paintings, stories. This game will forever be ingrained in my mind as one of the best.

The future

As covid raged, there I was in my house playing the future. I didn't get the exposure to the early half-life games as a lot of pc gamers did, but goddamn, the universe created here feels authentic and the medium, VR, is just another level. I can't wait for more experiences like this.
